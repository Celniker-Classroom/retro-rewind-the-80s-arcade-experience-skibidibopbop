Happy Spaghetti

Inspirational Writeup

The inspiration for our game was the arcade classic, “Nibbler,” which was released by Rock-Ola in 1982. It featured a simple pixelated snake that ate dots while navigating through a maze and avoiding its own tail. In our game, “Happy Spaghetti,” we used the same game mechanics in which the snake gains a body segment for each item it eats and dies if it hits itself. However, we also added some unique elements, such as making it spaghetti themed: the snake is one long pasta noodle, and each food item is a meatball. Our game also speeds up for every five meatballs that are eaten, which helps to increase the difficulty.

How to Play

Controls:

  • Arrow Keys: Move the snake
  • WASD Keys: Alternative controls
  • Space: Restart game after game over

Objective:

  • Eat meatballs to grow your spaghetti
  • Avoid hitting the walls
  • Don't collide with your own body
  • Get the highest score!

Tips:

  • The game speeds up as you eat more meatballs
  • Your high score is saved locally
  • Plan your moves ahead to avoid dead ends
